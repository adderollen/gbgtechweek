AI is more than hopes and dreams – meet the people who does it for real

Thursday 24/05 - 16:30 - 20:00

Venue: Convendum

Join now

We are sick and tired of all the talk about AI changing the world in 10–20 years. We’re here to talk about what’s happening right now. What are other companies working on? What can you do? Why should you do it in the first place?

To answer these questions we’ve invited three of Gothenburgs leading companies in the field to spill their secrets.

Precisely is taking the legal industry into the 21st century with it’s software solutions and contract management Saas-platform.

Einride is the Gothenburg based company making self-driving trucks in an attempt to crush Volvo, Daimler and even the entire railway system.

Stena is the 79 year old Swedish shipping company going head first into the future with a plan to be AI-supported in every process by 2021.

Doors open at 4:30 pm and the event starts at 5 pm. After a 45 minute panel talk you are all invited across the street for after work drinks hosted by Jansson & Norin. The doors close at 8 pm.

Curious about the venue? Check out this video: https://youtu.be/rNLwPiW8IJ0

Speakers:

  • Jonas de Lange, moderator and reporter at Breakit
  • Amer Mohammed, head of digital innovation at Stena
  • Nils-Erik Jansson, co-founder at Precisely
  • Tomas Ohlson, autonomous drive specialist at Einride
