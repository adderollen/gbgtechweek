We as the organizations in the IT domain, our teams and projects use latest technologies to solve real world issues. We want to take the opportunity during the tech week, to engage, educate and empower the community in Göteborg.

With the latest technologies like Blockchain and AI, there is usually denial or mis-conceptions. So, during the evening, we will bring industry leaders, entrepreneurs and innovators to spread knowledge and share their personal experiences.

This event will give you an opportunity to get to know what’s happening around and meet new people. The agenda of the event will be basic and is designed to encourage everyone to join irrelevant of their background.

So, join us in the after-work and let’s dive into the world of innovation-blockchain-sustainability.
