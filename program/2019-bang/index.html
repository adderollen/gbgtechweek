Keiretsu Forum for BANG #1

Thursday 9/5, 14:00 - 18:30

Venue: Adfenix, Vallgatan 14

Signup to the event!

We want to invite Gothenburg based business angels to take this opportunity to join the BANG (Business Angels of Gothenburg) network and get introduced to the Keiretsu model.

This is the first event in the BANG-series of events. As an investor and business angel you will have the chance to connect with other investors and meet selected startups on events every other month.

The event is built on the Keiretsu method: Five pitching startups รก 10 min pitch. 10 min Q&A/feedback. 10 min mindshare among investors.

Will food be served? No.
