Friday 25/05 - 14:00 - 15:30

How do you shape your startup to scale? How do you future-proof your business for what’s to come? Together with our panel we will dig deep into their experiences. Our experienced panel that will take you through this afternoon consists of:

  • Daniel Langkilde - Annotell
  • Gabriel Kamienny - Adfenix
  • Gustav von Sydow - Burt
  • John Eskilsson - Skyltmax
  • Jonas Karles - Minna Technologies

Join us this afternoon! Get to know our panelists, listen to their scale-up experiences, gain new insights, and of course ask a lot of questions.

So, let’s talk about scale!
