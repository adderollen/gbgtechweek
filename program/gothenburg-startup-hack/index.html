Program Gothenburg Startup Hack

Gothenburg Startup Hack

Saturday 19/05 - 08:00 - 23:59

Venue: Skansen Lejonet

Signup now

It's happening again! Gothenburg Startup Hack is returning after a few years of crazy places we will stay in a secure location. The hack will be in a castle! Bring your ideas to life on the top of the hill, within meter thick walls, the most prestigious place of the 17th century in Gothenburg.

The event is free and the signup is open and stays open until May 1th! We will select the best teams for the hack. The hack goes on from 8 am to 6 pm.

Process:
We will choose ~100 hackers from applicants. After 10 hours of building the product the hackers will vote for the 10 best teams who will pitch later.

Prices:
A jury will choose a winner from the perspective of Design, Tech and vision. The hackers vote will generate one winner of the hackers price. The pitches (among the 10 teams pitching) will have a vote where the best pitch is chosen.

Want to find other team members, join the matchup space

Gentlepeople, form your teams and start your thought engines! Claim your place in the castle!

After 6pm there will be a epic party all night. RSVPs will open when the signup closes.
