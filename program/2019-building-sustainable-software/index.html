Open source is praised for saving substantial resources you would have otherwise spent on developing and integrating applications developed by others. While many are aware of the risks related to Open Source Software (OSS), few are able to identify and handle those risks in a sustainable manner. During the workshop you will learn how to tackle compliance and security vulnerability risks in your products. The workshop will be interesting to all, entrepreneurs, programmers, CEOs, CTOs, compliance specialists, students and everyone with the passion to build innovative software products.
