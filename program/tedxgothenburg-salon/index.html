TEDxGothenburg Salon - Virtual & Augmented Reality

Thursday 24/05 - 17:30 - 21:00

Venue: The Techno Creatives, Geijersgatan 1 B

Signup has closed!

On the 24th of May, TEDxGöteborg welcomes you to its first Salon event of 2018 - an evening where we tackle mind-boggling questions about Virtual Reality alongside our invited panel member and with you at The Techno Creatives. Don't miss out on this one and come and join the discussion and mingle afterward. Drinks and snacks will be served. The entrance is for free.

What is TEDxGöteborgSalon?
TEDxSalon is an event by TEDxGöteborg, independently and locally organised, four times per year. Live speakers are invited and a smaller format allows interesting discussion to continue in person – each time around a new topic.

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TEDxGöteborg is a local self-organised community that brings people together to share a TED-like experience.

A big thank you to our friends The Techno Creatives!
