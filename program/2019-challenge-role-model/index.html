Challenge the image of role model in technology

Wednesday 8/5, 14:00 - 15:15

Venue: Dome of Visions

Signup to the event!

A day event focused on equality

Women in Tech Gothenburg and Dome of Visions invites the hardest working local organizations in the field of equality to share their perspective on sustainability during GbgTechWeek.

Pepp welcomes you to a panel talk with focus on creating role models for broadening the view of technology. Why is it important with role models in tech focused environments? Join and let's have a discussion.

Pepp aim at giving room for female role models in the technology and engineering world to trigger the interest, challenge prejudice and show the endless possibilities that the industry has to offer..
