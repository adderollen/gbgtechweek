Program CompInn - Competence in innovation

CompInn - Competence in innovation

Wednesday 23/05 - 12:00 - 16:00

Venue: Vasaparken

Signup now!

CompInn is the event that puts innovation and competence in focus. A forum for people with different expertise to meet and share their knowledge. Give start-ups a new way to display their businesses and find new colleagues. Maybe you want to join a start-up? Or is your start-up looking for new competencies? Either way, joining this event will give you an insight to the start-up landscape in West Sweden, more knowledge about the cool start-ups that are active here and of course tons of inspiration.

During CompInn you will have the chance to meet with actors in the innovation system, entrepreneurs and start-ups. There will also be inspiring speakers, talking about competence in different forms. And not to forget a rare opportunity for one-to-one meetings hosted by Venture Cup.
