The Blockchain Track

Tuesday 22/05 - 13:00 - 15:00

Venue: Visual Arena

Join now!

Welcome to the blockchain-track of gbgtechweek. Ethereum Gotheburg with the support of our sponsor, Business region Gothenburg proudly presents a wide range of companies, both domestic and international.

Ethereum Gothenburg started as a meetup for anyone interested in building the new economy that is native to the internet. Using new emerging technologies such as Ethereum and other blockchains, we can build various types of decentralized applications that enhance the trustworthiness of both offline and online communities, resulting in a more fair and transparent world.
