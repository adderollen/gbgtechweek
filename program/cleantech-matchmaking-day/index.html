Program Cleantech matchmaking day

Cleantech matchmaking day

Thursday 24/05 - 10:00 - 17:00

Venue: Vera's Grästmatta

Join now

Cleantech matchmaking day is the event where big meets small, corporates meet startups. Between 10-15 big companies together with 40-50 startups will meet and mingle on this day, hoping to find new, exciting opportunities for establishing partnerships or collaborations. Since “innovate or die” is all the rage right now, which emphasizes the need for corporates to stay innovative, we believe this event and the startups can aid in this mission. Because of this we have planned this event a little differently - where we put startups in control – putting the corporates on stage letting them pitch why startups should partner up with them. Once all the big companies have finished their presentation, the vote is on for the “best innovation partner”, which is decided by voting. Following that is the speed-dating session, where the participating startups get to pick what companies to talk to in the afternoon. More detailed program is found below:

Moderator intro: 10:00 - 10:20
Pitch competition for BIG companies: 10:20 - 12:00 - ”Best innovation partner for cleantech startups”
Lunch: 12:00 - 13:00 (included)
Keynote: 13:00 - 13:30 (TBA)
Speed dating: 13:30 - 17:00 – BIG companies and cleantech startups
Cleantech afterparty: 17:30 – 22:00 – Food, beverage, live music and a fun surprise (!)
