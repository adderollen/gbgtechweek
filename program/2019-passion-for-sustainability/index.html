Program Passion for sustainability – investors and entrepreneurs co-creating the assessment criteria

Passion for sustainability – investors and entrepreneurs co-creating the assessment criteria

Friday 10/5 - 09:00 - 12:00

Venue: Ekocentrum, Aschebergsgatan 44

Signup to the event!

As business angels, From IDA InWest wants, we want to ensure your business is investment-worthy in even 5 years from now. Start now! Join! Join our dynamic workshop ‘Passion for Sustainability’ and stay relevant for investors now, tomorrow and even in 5 years!

Global investment company BlackRock believes that in 5 years’ time, businesses who have not incorporated sustainability in their soul, will not be considered an attractive investment option.

The business must be sustainable from the entrepreneur herself all the way through the company’s value chain.

For large companies, sustainability assessment criteria have been defined and even determined by law. For small and medium-sized companies however, such a framework is lacking. Nevertheless, these are the companies who have a chance of getting it right from the beginning. And with the right set of assessment on board, the prerequisites for growth are set.

In ‘Passion for Sustainability’ we define together how to measure the performance of the company. Eventually this will result in how attractive you become for to potential investors.

The workshop explains how to incorporate sustainability into your business in a natural way: without it just being about ticking boxes. We share tips and tricks so that you can about sustainability on a business level make sustainability into your competitive advantage from now onhere on after!

If you want your business idea to be considered sustainable, and you want to contribute to the assessment criteria and continue to be attractive to potential investors, then‘Passion for Sustainability’ is the perfect workshop for you!

See you soon!
