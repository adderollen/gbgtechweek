Program Legal advice in a sustainable way

Legal advice in a sustainable way

Thursday 9/5, 16:30-19:00

Venue: Östra Hamngatan 29

Signup to the event!

For companies wishing to grow in a sustainable way, it is important to consider the law – not least for tech companies. However, the law today is complex and receiving legal counsel can often be expensive. How should one prioritize smartly to not drown in either expensive lawyer fees or lawsuits due to not taking the law in to consideration from the beginning? What are the advantages of having your own lawyer compared to an attorney?

Do not miss this exciting event where you gain practical tips and advice on how different issues can be prioritized, and advice on which pitfalls should be avoided by companies wishing to grow in a sustainable way. We conclude with an AW and mingle where you have the opportunity to ask all legal questions that you may have!
