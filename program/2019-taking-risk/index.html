Program Why taking risks is important for growth and requires sustainability

Why taking risks is important for growth and requires sustainability

Tuesday 7/5, 17:00 - 19:30

Venue: Biner

Signup to the event!

Marie (a Biner Consultant) and the Biner Founders will share personal stories and specific key lessons learned which enabled sustainability and business/career growth, through taking risks.

Marie will share stories from her career journey and from her current assignment on how through having a sustainable environment she is able to (continue) take growth inspiring risks for her client.

Key factors such as having a clear vision, dream and passion for one’s life creates a platform of sustainability which allows risk taking.

From an employer perspective we will discuss the importance of having a sustainable foundation when specific key risks are taken. We will also highlight how taking those key risks, maintained change, produced growth and inspired consultants, including hiring a wider range of consultants.

Agenda:

  • 17:00 - 17:30: Mingle (drinks / finger foods)
  • 17:30 - 17:45: Welcome and introductions
  • 17:45 – 18:45: Consultant/panel presentation
  • 18:45 – 19:00: Pause
  • 19:00 – 19:15: Questions from the audience
  • 19:15 – onwards: Drinks

Will food be served? Yes, Finger food and drinks.
