West Swedish Incubators and Science Parks, including the #greenUps network, welcome you to one of the hottest events presenting companies of the future, Compinn.

Are you interested in what the new up and coming companies are working on? Are you curious of current job opportunities or are you perhaps already looking for a new position? Or maybe you just want to be inspired and expand your network?
