Program Innovation - Corporates and Startups in collaboration

Innovation - Corporates and Startups in collaboration

Tuesday 22/05 - 10:00 - 12:00

Venue: Universeum

Join now

Join us for an intense session on corporate + start-up collaboration. We have invited some of the Swedish large industrial players working hands on with this today to share some of their insights; Peter Löfgren, head of ABB Synerleap and Magnus Viström, Innovation Manager, driving the SCA accelerator. Together with Stina Lantz, program manager B2B Startup Matchmaking program Ignite Sweden, Erik-Wilhelm Graaf Beem from BRG and Clementina from GU Ventures, we will discuss the following topics:

- How can corporates and startups collaborate?
- Why is it important?
- How to tackle the cultural differences corporate + startup?
- What are the keys to success?
- How to get started?

-> Are you a startup interested in corporate cooperation? Stina Lantz from Ignite will be available after the event to answer all your questions about how to join Ignite Sweden.

Speakers:

  • Peter Löfgren - Head of Synerleap powered by ABB
  • Magnus Viström - Innovation Manager, SCA
  • Erik-Wilhelm Graef Behm - Area & Investment Manager ICT, Business Region Göteborg ICT
  • Stina Lantz - Program Manager, Ignite Sweden
  • Klementina Österberg - CEO, GU Ventures AB
