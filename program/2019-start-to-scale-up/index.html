Deep learning for perception in autonomous vehicles -from research to product

An introduction to the work done at Zenuity within the field of deep learning for perception and experiences from the day-to-day work of trying to bring deep learning from research to product.

Zenuity delivers spearhead solutions to be the go-to, future-proof partner and holistic provider for ADAS and AD full software stack. We are currently the only company to date to balance robust solutions at the forefront of innovation in the AD space while enabling our clientele the flexibility to cherry pick, continuously scale up and freely integrate with the Zenuity software. We are building a modular ADAS and AD systems that can be scaled from vehicles that need cost-efficient NCAP solution, to vehicles that need the latest and greatest autonomous driving features.

Eskil and Vilhelm have been working with deep learning for perception at Zenuity since the company started in 2017. They will give an introduction to the work done at Zenuity within this field and share their experience of day-to-day work, trying to bring deep learning from research to product. Eskil in the capacity of industrial PhD student and Vilhelm as a development engineer.
