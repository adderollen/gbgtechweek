Program Start to Scale up; how to accelerate your business

Start to Scale up; how to accelerate your business

Wednesday 8/5, 08:00 - 09:15

Venue: Dome of Visions

Signup to the event!

A day event focused on equality

Women in Tech Gothenburg and Dome of Visions invites the hardest working local organizations in the field of equality to share their perspective on sustainability during GbgTechWeek.

Women in Tech have invited Chalmers Ventures and Startup Sweden, Tillväxtverket who will discuss Start to Scale up; how to accelerate your business or business idea. Are you thinking about starting your own tech-business? Or do you run a tech-biz today but need help in scaling it up? This event is a meeting point for all entrepreneurs - no matter what stage you're in.

The morning starts with some coffee and mingle followed by a presentation, by Malin Frithiofsson from UBI top world ranked tech incubator Chalmers Ventures, about Chalmers Venture's 5 different programs for startups and their offers to early-stage entrepreneurs. After this, Isa Nordeld from Startup Sweden, Tillväxtverket will talk about Startup Sweden's Femtech Boot Camp for women founders and about their New York Boot Camp for scale-ups looking to expand to the UK.

The morning event ends with a fun & interactive workshop with the focus on getting to know new people and help each other accelerating your business - whatever stage you are in today.

Will food be served? No.
