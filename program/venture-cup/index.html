Program Venture Cup Regional Final

Venture Cup Regional Final

Wednesday 16/05 - 16:30 - 21:30

Venue: Elite Park Avenue Hotel

Signup now!

Venture Cup turns celebrate 20 years and you are welcome to join us in the celebrations. On the 16th of May it's time for this year's regional final. During the evening we will present the winners in the Startup competition, and bring you inspiration and interviews. You will also, during this unforgettable evening, join in on Venture Cup's journey from the beginning in 1998 up until toady. On this journey we will put light on some of the success stories that Venture Cup has been involved in.

Gabriel Kamienny, co-founder of Adfenix, is the speaker of the evening and will give you an unique insight in their journey towards becoming Sweden's next unicorn.

Secure your seat and join us at this night filled with exiting entrepreneurial stories, fruitful networking, and be first in line to hear tomorrow's entrepreneurs.
