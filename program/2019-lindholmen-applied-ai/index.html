Program Lindholmen Applied AI

Lindholmen Applied AI

Tuesday 7/5, 13:30 - 18:00

Venue: Lindholmen Conference Center

Signup to the event!

Artificial intelligence is the buzzword of the moment and we are talking about AI as a important part of “the fourth industrial revolution”. AI is increasingly being absorbed and adopted into just about every business sector, but how is it being applied today? Will it change our society? How are businesses using AI to tackle real business problems and where are we really in the development phase of AI?  

Lindholmen Applied AI is a half day conference where leading businesses from different industries will present and talk about how they are applying and using AI today, going beyond the hype of artificial intelligence and instead talking about real applications and existing solutions.
