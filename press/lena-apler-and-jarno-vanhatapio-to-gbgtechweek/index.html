Super business angel Lena Apler and serial entrepreneur Jarno Vanhatapio to #gbgtechweek

The two rockstars, super business angel and founder of Collector Lena Apler, and the serial entrepreneur behind Nelly.com and NA-KD, Jarno Vanhatapio, will attend #gbgtechweek. On Lindholmen Connected Lena will talk about the steps that are needed to move from innovation to business. Jarno will be hosted by Almi Företagspartner, Grant Thornton & Women in Tech and talk on the subject of digital tech and how to create online attraction.

“We love the mixture of events where some attract successful entrepreneurs such as Lena and Jarno, and some focus on hands-on guiding for enthusiasts during their first steps on becoming the next generation of tech-superstars.” - Malin Kjällström, Organizer of #gbgtechweek

Earlier this week the first five events were released. Now the next five events are presented.

What is an app and how do I make one? with Craft Academy
Come spend an evening with Craft Academy and make your first web app! We’ll walk you through what makes an app different from a web site, then we’ll work together to build your first app: an online address book.

Gothenburg Startup Landscape with Venture Cup
Venture Cup & SEB proudly presents an overview of the startup ecosystem, in collaboration with various important organizations and companies from all over Gothenburg, to offer you information, guidance and great advice about how you can develop your business idea and take it to the next level. The signup open tomorrow on the April 1st.

Idea Generation Workshop with Chalmers School of Entrepreneurship
Want to find your idea? This is a guided workshop where the serial entrepreneur Alexander Mafi will teach you about idea generation tools and how to boost your creativity.

Digital tech - Online Attraction with ALMI Företagspartner
ALMI Företagspartner along with Grant Thornton and Women In Tech Network is presenting the serial entrepreneur Jarno Vanhatapio, Karolina Brorsson and Marketing Automation expert Anthony Seymour. Together they will talk about digital tech and how to create online attraction.

Lindholmen Connected Business Network with Lindholmen Science Park
Welcome to Lindholmen Connected with the business profile and super business angel Lena Apler as keynote speaker. We will hear her talk on the subject of the interplay of innovation and investment - which forces are needed to move from innovation to business?

Stay tuned for the upcoming events which will be released soon!
