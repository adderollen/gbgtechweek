The Swedish Government’s Startup Director and the success story CELLINK join #gbgtechweek!

Marie Wall, Startup Director at the Swedish Government, attend #gbgtechweek during the IPO-event where Nasdaq and AktieTorget will present their stock exchanges together with the awesome Swedish ecosystem for small cap listings. They will be joined by Klementina Österberg, CEO of GU Ventures, who have listed many companies and entrepreneur Gusten Danielsson, who will tell you about the success story of CELLINK.

"Each year #gbgtechweek attract more and more inspiring persons. We think it is important that the #gbgtech community can gain this inspiration in order to push themselves to strive towards being the next success story." - Malin Kjällström, Organizer of #gbgtechweek

This batch of events does not only focus on success, but also the importance of failure. One Stop Future Shop will through their event stress that failing is a part of entrepreneurship, and how you deal with it. These are the three events in this batch:

FailureFestival by One Stop Future Shop
On stage successful entrepreneurs will share their stories of the times they messed up or things went really bad – and what they learned from it. How do failure make us better entrepreneurs?

Startups going public with GU Ventures and partners
GU Ventures and partners would like to invite entrepreneurs, investors and all of you, who are interested in learning more about the IPO-process, to a half day event. The event will be opened by Nasdaq ringing the bell in the morning and opening their Stock Exchange for the day from the event, followed by AktieTorget and it will be closed by Marie Wall, the Swedish Government's Start-up Director. There will be presentations from an impressive list of expert speakers and some listed companies sharing their stories, such as CELLINK. The event also aims to bring together companies with investors and organizations.

Gothenburg’s dance with China with GU Ventures and partners
GU Ventures and partners would like to invite all entrepreneurs, investors, business executives and all of you, who are interested in learning more about the Chinese market, to a half day event. There will be presentations from an impressive list of expert speakers from GU Ventures’ partners, the Swedish Embassy in Beijing, China Telecom, CEVT, GU School of Executive Education, Oboya, Bluetest and the fund Nordic Growth Technogy Partners.

Stay tuned for the upcoming events which will be released soon!
