#gbgtechweek 2018 is ready to launch!

#gbgtechweek is back with more events than ever! In our fourth edition, #gbgtechweek is bringing back the format that you know already to gather the nordic tech and startup community in a week full of inspiring events. The tech and startup week is set to take place on May 21st - May 25th. Interest from organizers have gone through the roof, bringing new formats for events and a full week of activities for everyone; from the tech-savvy visitor, to the future entrepreneur! The 2018 #gbgtechweek edition will be epic.

After another successful year, the focus of #gbgtechweek 2018 is to explore and bring light to all the technology areas shaping the future. To do so, stand-alone events and full days of activities will run through the week. These events will be focus either in tech-specific industry, or in stage-specific for incoming startups.

”This year we want to include more startups and people from our neighboring regions; facilitating the collaboration between industry and tech-intense companies. Our objective is to build bridges between the technology clusters of gothenburg with the entire national and international sphere” - Linus Olsson, Co-founder & Organiser of #gbgtechweek.

We are adding new events to the program, which is available in our website, every week. To make life easier, all events are in chronological order and details of how to join is displayed under every event. Just scroll down to find what you are looking for, click to read the details, and sign up through the linked Eventbrite site to secure your place!

We will be announcing more details of each one of this year’s events soon, so stay tuned and make sure to save the dates. See you soon!
