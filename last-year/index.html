by Chalmers Ventures & Chalmers School of Entrepreneurship

After 2 years at Chalmers School of Entrepreneurship and a 10 months education and incubation process, these talented entrepreneurs are ready to show you their findings and tell you how they will tackle the next step in building a scalable business.
